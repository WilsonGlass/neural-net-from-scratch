Adding Layers
---

So far our nn has only had one layer. nns become "deep"
once we add 2 or more <b>hidden</b> layers.
At the moment we have just one layer which is effectively an
output layer.

To create a new layer, we are going to copy and paste
our _weights_ and _biases_ to
_weights2_ and biases2_ and change their values
to new made up sets.
